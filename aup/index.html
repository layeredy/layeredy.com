Users are expected to operate within reasonable usage limits as outlined in their service plans. Abusing resources to affect the performance of the service for other users is strictly prohibited.

If required by law, we may disclose user data to authorities in compliance with legal requests such as subpoenas, court orders, or other legal processes. Users are responsible for ensuring that their activities on our services comply with all local, national, and international laws and regulations.

We reserve the right to terminate any user account for any reason, including but not limited to violations of this AUP. Specifically, violations related to network disruption may incur a fee of up to $200. The severity of the violation and the impact on our services will determine the appropriate response, which may include:
  • Warning: For minor violations.
  • Temporary Suspension: For moderate violations.

  • Additional Fees: Up to $200 for network disruptions or impairment of service.
